Bollerslev, T., A. J. Patton, and R. Quaedvlieg, 2016, 
Exploiting the Errors: A Simple Approach for Improved Volatility Forecasting, 
Journal of Econometrics, 192, 1-18.

This folder contains the high-frequency volatility estimates to reproduce the paper. All estimates are based  on 5-minute returns from TAQ.
