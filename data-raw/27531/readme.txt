The do-file Replication.do produces the main Table (Table 1 and associated back-of-the-envelope calculations) and Figure (Figure A1) of the paper and the online Appendix. 

The program uses Stata 14 and later versions; the program requires installing the package rdrobust (https://rdpackages.github.io/rdrobust/) developed by Calonico et al. (2014).

The data underlying this article were provided by the Swiss State Secretariat of Economic Affairs (SECO) by permission. Data will be shared on request to the corresponding author with permission of the Swiss State Secretariat of Economic Affairs (SECO).

References: Calonico, Cattaneo and Titiunik (2014): Robust Data-Driven Inference in the Regression-Discontinuity Design. Stata Journal 14(4): 909-946.
